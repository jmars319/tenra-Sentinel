# Sentinel by Tenra Handoffs

Sentinel by Tenra stays unique as a shared signal and risk assessment app. Phone lookup is the first concrete use case, not the whole product boundary.

## Produces

- `tenra-sentinel.risk-brief.v1` for Derive, Guardrail, Assembly, or manual review.
- Markdown lookup reports with evidence, provenance, confidence, and action posture.

## Consumes

- Manual review signals.
- Future phone, email, entity, marketplace, moderation, and operations source observations.

Sentinel should not become a generic Q&A or final decision app. It gathers and weighs evidence; Derive explains conclusions and Guardrail reviews actions.
